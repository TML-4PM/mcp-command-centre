# Tenant Portfolio Widget

Provide a high-level operational and commercial view across all tenants.

## Table

| tenant | queries | blocked | REAL | PARTIAL | PRETEND | revenue |

## Data sources

- sql_execution_audit
- sql_reality_ledger
- tenant_usage

## Behaviour

- auto refresh
- sortable
- filter by tenant and plan

## Outcome

Used for:
- sales
- operations
- board reporting
