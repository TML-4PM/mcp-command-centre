The Thesis in One Paragraph

Every person has an ‘AI Sweet Spot’ — a combination of time-of-day, cognitive state, task type, and personal profile where AI assistance multiplies rather than diminishes their output. T4H is building the research infrastructure, assessment tools, and service layer to identify and exploit that sweet spot for underserved populations: neurodivergent individuals, elderly Australians, Indigenous communities, ESL speakers, and anyone navigating altered or non-standard cognitive states. The research is the moat. The products are the proof. The data is the asset.
